Is the cosmetic product a single or multi-component product?

Multi-component products are composed of several components that are not marketed separately. They are sold as a set and intended to be used as a mix or sequentially.

Examples of multi-component cosmetics include:

  • hair dye sets
  • skin peeling kits
  • permanent styling products
  • anti-age/revitalising kits
