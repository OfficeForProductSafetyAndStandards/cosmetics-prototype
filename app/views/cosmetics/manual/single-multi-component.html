Is the cosmetic product available as single item or as a kit?

Kits are composed of components that are not marketed separately. They are sold as a kit and intended to be used as a mix or sequentially.

Examples of multi-component cosmetic products include:

  • hair dye sets
  • skin peeling kits
  • permanent styling products

If the cosmetic product is sold as a gift set where the components are not mixed or used sequentially notify each product separately.
