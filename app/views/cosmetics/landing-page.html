Cosmetic products notified to the European Commission before the UK left the European Union

If a cosmetic product notification was submitted to the European Commission before the UK left the European Union, it can only continue to be sold in the UK if information about a new UK-based responsible person is provided within 60 days of the UK leaving the European Union.
