Without the CPNP product number, you’ll need to manually enter the details of the product

If you can find the product number, it will be much simpler to complete your product registration.

How to find your CPNP product number

  1. Log in to your CPNP account
  2. Under ‘Products’, click ‘My notifications’
  3. Find the notification for this product
    1. a. On the left side of the screen is ‘CPNP Ref.’ with a 7 digit number

If you have found the number, add your CPNP reference number to make notification simpler.
