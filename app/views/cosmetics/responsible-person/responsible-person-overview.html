UK responsible person

A responsible person can be an organisation or an individual, provided they are are based in the UK and are either the:

  • manufacturer of the cosmetic products
  • organisation for which the cosmetic products are manufactured, or
  • importer of the cosmetic products, if they are is manufactured outside the UK
  • a third-party given permission by either the manufacturer or importer
