Do you want to create a new UK Responsible Person?

The Responsible Person will be either the:

  • manufacturer of the cosmetic
  • the organisation it is manufactured for
  • the importer of the cosmetic, if it is manufactured outside the UK

A Responsible Person can be an organisation or an individual. The Responsible Person must:

  • ensure the cosmetic complies with relevant regulation
  • be based in the UK
