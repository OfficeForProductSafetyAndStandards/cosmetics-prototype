Register a cosmetic product for sale

If you sell cosmetics in the UK you must have a responsible organisation or person to keep the details of the product up-to-date.

Before you start

Products on sale in the UK and EU

If the product has been registered in the EU, you can use the product file from the Cosmetics Products Notification Portal (CPNP) to save time.

Products only on sale in the UK

To complete the registration of your product you will need:

  1. a detailed list of ingredients
  2. name, address and contact details of the responsible organisation or person
  3. a photo of the packaging
  4. a photo of the label

If your product contains nanomaterials

Cosmetic products containing nanomaterials must be notified to the Secretary of State by the responsible person by electronic means six months prior to being placed on the market. Unless they are in conformity with the requirements set out in Annex III.
