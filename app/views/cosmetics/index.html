Submit notifications for cosmetic products to be sold or given away in the UK

For safety, all cosmetic products available in the UK must have a notification with details of the product submitted and the responsible person.

Since the UK left the European Union (EU), all cosmetic products on sale or given away in the UK need to be notified in the UK. The process for new products and products that had been notified in the EU before Brexit and new cosmetic is the same, but you need to provide less information for products notified before Brexit. You do not have to submit notifications for cosmetic products discontinued before 29 March 2019.

New cosmetic product notifications in the UK

To submit a new cosmetic product notification you need to give details of:

  • the organisation or individual responsible for ensuring regulations are followed, known as the ‘responsible person’
  • a contact person to give information about the cosmetic products to the national poison information service or market surveillance authorities
  • the name of the cosmetic product
  • if applicable, the shades in which the cosmetic product is available
  • whether or not the cosmetic product contains carcinogenic, mutagenic or toxic for reproduction (CMR) substances
  • whether or not the cosmetic product contains nanomaterials
  • the formulation as either a frame formulation, ranges, or exact concentrations
  • an image of the label

Cosmetic products notified in the EU before 29 March 2019

To continue to sell or give away cosmetic products that were notified in the EU before Brexit, you must submit your product notifications in the UK within 90 days of the UK leaving the EU – that is by 11pm on 27 June 2019.

You need to same details as a new cosmetic product notification, but you do not need to provide:

  • whether or not the cosmetic product contains carcinogenic, mutagenic or toxic for reproduction (CMR) substances
  • whether or not the cosmetic product contains nanomaterials
  • an image of the label

How to submit cosmetic product notifications in the UK

Cosmetic products notified in the EU

The EU Cosmetic Product Notification Portal (CPNP) allows you to export ZIP files containing XML files for each notification.

You can upload notification files only as ZIP files containing XML files on the UK cosmetic product notification service.

To use the data from your EU notifications for UK notifications you need to:

  • export your data as ZIP files for all cosmetic products
  • upload your ZIP file containing XML files to the UK notification service
  • if the original EU notification file has the formulation as an attached document, you will also need add this

Cosmetic products that have not been notified in the EU

You can submit a cosmetic product notification by manually entering information about the product.

Cosmetics products containing nanomaterials

If a cosmetic product contains nanomaterials not already approved for use in cosmetic products, the responsible person must notify their proposed use 6 months before the cosmetic product containing them goes on sale or is given away.
