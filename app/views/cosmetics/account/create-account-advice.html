Create an account

To create an account you will need to provide information for the UK Responsible person and a contact details for a person if there is an health risk or harm is caused by your cosmetic.

If you are joining an existing responsible person, you must be invited by someone from that organisation.

For the UK responsible person you need their:

  • business number if the responsible person is a business
  • business name or full name
  • UK address
  • phone number
  • email address

For the the emergency contact person you need their:

  • full name
  • phone number
