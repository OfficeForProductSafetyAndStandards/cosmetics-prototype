The EU Cosmetic Product Notification Portal (CPNP) allows users to download a PDF and ZIP files containing XML files for each notification.

To use your EU notification files you must export your data as zip files for all cosmetic products.

Once you have uploaded your files you will need to add an image for each and if the original EU notification had attached a document with formulation information, you will also need provide this.
